- **Phantom Minions**: At certain stages, the boss summons phantom minions to assist in its attack, which can complicate the battle.

## How to Prepare for the Ghost Blade Fight

Before you engage in battle, preparation is critical. Here are a few tips to ensure you’re ready for the Ghost Blade’s onslaught:

- **Equip Light Armor**: The Ghost Blade’s attacks can be swift and difficult to dodge. Light armor allows you to move more freely and avoid damage.
- **Use Long-Range Weapons**: If you can’t get close to the boss, using long-range weapons like bows or magic will help you maintain a safe distance while dealing consistent damage.
- **Stock Up on Healing Items**: The fight can be drawn out, and you’ll want plenty of healing potions or food to keep your health up. Make sure to carry some of the best healing items you can find.

### Recommended Loadouts:

- **Healing Items**: Potions that regenerate health quickly, or food that provides buffs.

## The Fight: Strategies to Defeat Ghost Blade

To defeat the Ghost Blade, you’ll need to be patient, quick on your feet, and aware of its attack patterns. Here are some proven strategies:

1. **Dodge and Strike**: The key to surviving the Ghost Blade’s attacks is learning how to dodge effectively. Its movements are rapid, but with practice, you can time your dodges. Once you’ve dodged an attack, quickly counter with a few strikes.
   
2. **Focus on the Minions**: While the Ghost Blade is dangerous on its own, its phantom minions can be just as deadly. When they appear, focus on clearing them out first to reduce the number of threats in the area.

3. **Anticipate the Teleports**: The Ghost Blade can teleport unpredictably, but it always follows a pattern. It tends to teleport either behind you or directly to the side. Watch for its subtle movements and predict its next location.

4. **Avoid the AoE Attacks**: When the Ghost Blade raises its blade high, it’s preparing for an area-of-effect (AoE) attack. Quickly dodge out of range to avoid heavy damage.

5. **Stay Calm During Phase Transitions**: As the fight progresses, the Ghost Blade’s attacks will become more aggressive, and the minions will appear more frequently. Keep your focus and adapt to the new phase without panicking.
