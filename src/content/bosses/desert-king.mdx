## Desert King Fight Overview

The Desert King resides in the arid, treacherous deserts of *Where Winds Meet*, and players must navigate through sandstorms and hostile creatures to reach him. The fight itself involves a series of challenging phases where the Desert King uses a variety of devastating attacks. 

### Key Mechanics to Watch Out For

- **Sandstorm Phase**: The Desert King summons a powerful sandstorm that obscures vision, making it harder for players to track his movements. Be ready to rely on sound cues and careful positioning.
- **Poisonous Spikes**: The King frequently uses poison spikes that damage anyone who gets too close. Keep your distance when he raises his hand to prepare this attack.
- **Summon Minions**: Periodically, the Desert King will summon smaller enemies to distract you. These minions must be dealt with quickly to avoid being overwhelmed.

### Strategy Tips for Defeating the Desert King

1. **Stay Mobile**: Constant movement is key to surviving the Desert King’s attacks. The sandstorm will impair your ability to spot him, so make sure you're always on the move to avoid the poison spikes and his physical strikes.
2. **Use Ranged Attacks**: If possible, use ranged weapons or abilities to deal damage while keeping your distance from the boss. This reduces the risk of taking damage from his more direct attacks.
3. **Focus on Minions First**: When the Desert King summons minions, take them out quickly to minimize distractions. Letting them roam freely can quickly turn the tide of battle against you.
4. **Coordinate with Your Team**: If you’re fighting the Desert King in a group, ensure that everyone knows their role. Assign one or two players to deal with the minions while others focus on damaging the boss.
