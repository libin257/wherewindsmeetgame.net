### Frost Yaksha: Boss Overview

The Frost Yaksha is one of the more challenging bosses in *Where Winds Meet*. Known for its cold-themed abilities, this boss can freeze players in their tracks with its powerful ice attacks. Here’s a quick breakdown of what you can expect during the battle:

- **Location**: Frost Yaksha can be found in the northern part of the map, where the cold winds blow and snowstorms are a constant threat.
- **Type**: Elemental Boss (Ice)
- **Difficulty**: Hard
- **Recommended Level**: 50+

#### Key Abilities of Frost Yaksha:
- **Icy Breath**: Deals heavy damage and applies a freezing debuff.
- **Frost Slam**: A devastating ground-pound attack that can shatter shields and freeze players in a wide radius.
- **Summon Ice Minions**: The boss occasionally summons ice minions to aid in battle, making crowd control essential.

### Preparation Tips for the Frost Yaksha Fight

Before heading into the battle, make sure you’re fully prepared. Here's how you can boost your chances of victory:

- **Bring Heat-Resistant Gear**: Equip armor or accessories that protect against freezing effects. This will help mitigate the Frost Yaksha’s debuffs.
- **Have Healing Potions Ready**: The Frost Yaksha can deal substantial damage quickly, so healing potions are essential to sustain you through the fight.
- **Strong DPS and Crowd Control**: Since the boss summons minions, ensure you have at least one member in your party with good area-of-effect (AoE) skills or crowd control abilities.

### How to Defeat Frost Yaksha

Now that you're prepared, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to tackle the Frost Yaksha boss.

#### 1. **Dodge the Icy Breath**
The Frost Yaksha’s Icy Breath is one of its most dangerous attacks, capable of freezing you in place and dealing massive damage. To avoid this, make sure to keep moving and use your dodge ability at the right moment to avoid being caught in its blast.

#### 2. **Avoid the Frost Slam**
When the boss begins to charge up for a Frost Slam, it’s crucial to position yourself outside of its range. This attack has a large AoE, so make sure your team spreads out to avoid multiple players being hit at once.

#### 3. **Deal with Ice Minions Quickly**
Once the Frost Yaksha summons minions, prioritize clearing them out before focusing back on the boss. Their attacks may seem weak on their own, but when combined with the boss’s ice abilities, they can overwhelm unprepared players.

#### 4. **Use Fire or Heat-Based Attacks**
If you’re using elemental abilities, fire or heat-based attacks are particularly effective against the Frost Yaksha. Use your fire abilities to deal extra damage and negate some of its ice-based attacks.

#### 5. **Timing Your Attacks**
Patience is key during this fight. Don’t rush in with all your attacks at once. Wait for the Frost Yaksha to finish one of its big moves (like the Icy Breath or Frost Slam) before striking hard. Timing is crucial to avoid taking unnecessary damage.
