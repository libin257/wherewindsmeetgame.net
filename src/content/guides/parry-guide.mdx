## What Is a Parry?

Parrying is a defensive technique that allows you to **deflect an incoming attack** at the precise moment the enemy strike connects. A successful parry:

- Cancels the enemy attack  
- Staggers the attacker  
- Opens a counter window  
- Deals posture damage  
- Prevents all incoming damage  

Perfect parrying is more rewarding but requires strict timing and focus.
