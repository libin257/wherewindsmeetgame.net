## Introduction to Stealth

Stealth in *Where Winds Meet* combines wuxia agility, environmental awareness, and assassination tools to create a smooth infiltration system. You can:

- Sneak past guards  
- Assassinate enemies silently  
- Hide in environmental objects  
- Use disguises to infiltrate camps  
- Distract enemies using sound or thrown objects  
- Disable lights or find shadows for cover  

This **where winds meet stealth** system reflects Chinese martial arts fantasy while offering modern stealth mechanics.

# Core Stealth Techniques

## 1. **Crouch Movement**
Walking while crouched is the foundation of stealth. This mode:

- Reduces sound  
- Lowers your silhouette  
- Makes you harder to detect from a distance  

## 2. **Assassinations**
Approach from behind and perform a silent takedown.

- Instantly eliminates most human enemies  
- Completely silent  
- Cannot assassinate some elite armored enemies  

## 3. **Environmental Hiding**
You can hide inside:

- Grass  
- Bushes  
- Behind crates  
- Behind walls and pillars  
- On rooftops  

Great for controlling guard patterns.

## 4. **Rooftop Infiltration**
The wuxia movement system allows:

- Roof-running  
- Wall climbing  
- Air-dashes  
- Gliding into enemy camps  

Vertical infiltration is one of the strongest parts of the **where winds meet stealth** system.

# Stealth Combat Flow

A refined **where winds meet stealth** approach follows this pattern:

1. Scout the area from a rooftop  
2. Mark patrol routes  
3. Eliminate isolated guards first  
4. Use distractions to split groups  
5. Assassinate priority targets  
6. Move through shadows  
7. Escape to rooftops if compromised  

This playstyle rewards patience and observation.

# Stealth vs. Direct Combat

### Stealth Advantages:
- Zero resource cost  
- Quick elimination of targets  
- Avoids large group fights  
- Enables infiltration-focused quests  
- Provides unique assassination animations  

### Stealth Limitations:
- Not all enemies can be assassinated  
- Many bosses require direct combat  
- Detection leads to heavy reinforcement waves  

Still, stealth is powerful when used correctly.

# Tips for Effective Stealth Play

- Always crouch unless sprinting to reposition.  
- Stay above enemies ‚Äî rooftops are your best friend.

- Combine stealth with poison or bleed tools for silent kills.
