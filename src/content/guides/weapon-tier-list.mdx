This **where winds meet weapon tier list** ranks every major weapon category in *Where Winds Meet* based on DPS output, ease of use, survivability, stagger potential, elemental synergy, and late-game performance. Because the game allows weapon swapping, every weapon has viable use cases — but some clearly outperform others in most activities.

---

## Tier List Overview

Weapons are ranked across four difficulty formats:

- **Solo Bossing**
- **Mob Clearing**
- **PvP Performance**
- **Beginner-Friendliness**

These rankings reflect overall strength and consistency.

### **Tier S** — Overpowered, top performance  
### **Tier A** — Excellent and reliable  
### **Tier B** — Good but situational  
### **Tier C** — Niche or outclassed  

Let’s dive into the complete **where winds meet weapon tier list**.

---


## **Nameless Sword / Light Blade**
*Role: Fast DPS, crit builds*

### Why It’s S Tier:
- Fastest parry recovery  
- High crit synergy  
- Tight dodge-cancel windows  
- Amazing boss DPS  
- Low stamina cost  

This weapon consistently tops the **where winds meet weapon tier list** for players who enjoy agile combat.

---

## **Stormbreaker Spear**
*Role: Mid-range DPS + lightning burst*

### Why It’s S Tier:
- Great spacing control  
- Excellent interrupt tools  
- Lightning scaling is top-tier  
- Extremely safe vs. bosses  

Strong in almost every scenario.

---

## **Panacea Fan (Bleed Build)**
*Role: Status DPS / hybrid utility*

### Why It’s S Tier:
- Insane bleed stacking  
- Safe mid-range pokes  
- Strong poison/DoT synergy  
- Works in both PvE and PvP  

The DoT pressure elevates it to S-tier dominance.

---


## **Long Spear**
*Role: Tank/DPS hybrid*

### Why It’s A Tier:
- Great control  
- Consistent stagger  
- Safe spacing  
- Works well for beginners  

Excellent for defensive playstyles.

---

## **Iron Flute**
*Role: Support / hybrid control*

### Why It’s A Tier:
- Long-range AoE waves  
- Useful buffs/debuffs  
- Versatile team utility  

Not raw DPS, but insanely valuable in group content.

---

## **Dual Blades**
*Role: Frenetic DPS*

### Why It’s A Tier:
- Fastest multi-hit chains  
- Perfect for Wind/lightning builds  
- High aggression potential  

Falls off slightly in posture damage, but still strong.

---


## **Meteor Hammer**
*Role: Heavy stagger / AoE burst*

### Why It’s B Tier:
- Slow animations  
- High skill requirement  
- Destroyer against large mobs  

Great when mastered, but punishing for mistiming.

---

## **Greatsword**
*Role: Defensive bruiser*

### Why It’s B Tier:
- Great tank potential  
- High single-hit damage  
- Poor mobility  

More niche but excellent in specific tanky builds.

---


*(Note: Nothing in *Where Winds Meet* is “bad.” Tier C simply includes weapons overshadowed by others.)*

## **Basic Fans (Non-Bleed Builds)**
*Role: Utility-focused*

### Why It’s C Tier:
- Low scaling without bleed/poison  
- Outperformed by Panacea Fan  

Still viable for stylish or support playstyles.

---

## **Uninfused Early-Game Weapons**
### Why They’re Here:
- Outclassed quickly  
- Minimal scaling  
- No elemental synergy  

Useful for leveling, but not competitive long-term.

### **Best DPS:**

### **Best Tank:**

### **Best Control/Support:**

### **Best Beginner Weapons:**  
- Long Spear (A)  
- Nameless Sword (S)

This **where winds meet weapon tier list** highlights the current meta strengths of each weapon type. Balance changes may shift rankings, but overall:

- Swords and lightning spears dominate DPS  
- Fans rule status builds  
- Spears and flutes excel in control  
- Heavy weapons remain powerful but timing-dependent  

Try multiple weapons — the game encourages experimentation, and each style offers its own martial-arts flavor.
