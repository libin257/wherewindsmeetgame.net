## How Fast Travel Works

Fast travel in *Where Winds Meet* is based on **unlocking map points by discovery**. These points include:

- Waypoints  
- Shrines  
- Villages and towns  
- Major landmarks  
- Sect headquarters  
- Mountain peaks  
- Bridges and trading outposts  

Once unlocked, you can teleport to these points instantly from your world map.

The **where winds meet fast travel** system is designed to reward exploration while reducing unnecessary backtracking.

---


Fast travel points are unlocked simply by **approaching** them. Once you get close enough, the icon becomes active on your map.
