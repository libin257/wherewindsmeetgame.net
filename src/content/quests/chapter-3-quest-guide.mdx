## Key Tips for Success in Chapter 3

The challenges in Chapter 3 can be difficult, but with the right approach, you can overcome them. Here are a few strategies that will make your journey through this chapter much smoother:

The Ice Crystal puzzle in Chapter 3 is one of the more challenging obstacles players will face. The puzzle requires players to activate a series of levers in a specific order, but the correct sequence is hidden in the surrounding environment.

The solution to the puzzle involves looking at the surrounding ice formations. Each formation has a distinct color, and players must pull the levers in the order that corresponds to these colors. It's a tricky puzzle, but players have found success by carefully observing their surroundings.
