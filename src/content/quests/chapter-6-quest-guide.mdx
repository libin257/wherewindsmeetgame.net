### Tips to Conquer the Frost Lord

Defeating the Frost Lord is one of the toughest parts of Chapter 6. Here are a few tips based on player experience to help you out:

- **Use Fire-Based Attacks**: The Frost Lord is particularly vulnerable to fire, so equip your character with fire-based abilities or weapons to deal massive damage. If you have a fire mage or archer, now is the time to let them shine.
- **Dodge and Counter**: The Frost Lord’s attacks can deal significant damage, so be sure to time your dodges perfectly. Once you dodge, counter with your most powerful abilities for the best effect.
- **Watch Out for Minions**: The Frost Lord summons minions during the fight. While focusing on the Frost Lord, don't forget to deal with these minions to avoid being overwhelmed.

Community reports indicate that the Frost Lord’s second phase becomes particularly challenging. Having healing potions and defensive abilities is highly recommended for this stage.
