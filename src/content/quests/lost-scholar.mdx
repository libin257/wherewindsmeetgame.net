## How to Start the Lost Scholar Side Quest

The *Lost Scholar* side quest can be started early in your journey, but it does require a bit of exploration. According to community feedback, this quest is often initiated by speaking with NPCs in the *Valley of Wisdom*. Here, you’ll find a scholar who has lost critical documents that hold the key to an ancient mystery.

### Key Steps to Begin:
- **Location**: Head to the *Valley of Wisdom*.
