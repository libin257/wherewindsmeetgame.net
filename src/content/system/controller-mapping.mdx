## Does the Game Support Full Controller Mapping?

Yes.  
*Where Winds Meet* includes **full controller remapping**, allowing you to:

- Rebind every action  
- Customize combat shortcuts  
- Create alternate presets  
- Adjust aim/turn sensitivity  
- Change dodge/lock-on behavior  
- Swap button layouts for left-handed or claw grip players  

This makes controller play highly flexible and comfortable.
