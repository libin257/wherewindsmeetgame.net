This **where winds meet panacea fan build** guide focuses on one of the most unique hybrid weapons in *Where Winds Meet*: the Panacea Fan. Unlike purely offensive weapons, the Panacea Fan blends elegant martial arts, support skills, and poison/bleed mechanics to create a flexible, mobility-focused build with surprising sustained DPS.

## Introduction

The Panacea Fan is a lightweight but deadly weapon that combines:

- Long-range fan strikes  
- Bleed stacking  
- Utility support (buffs & debuffs)  
- Agile dodge-cancel combat  
- Poison synergy  
- Crowd-control zoning  

When optimized for bleed damage, a **where winds meet panacea fan build** can shred enemies through stacking DoT effects while maintaining exceptional safety and mobility.

---


Players who choose this build typically enjoy:

- Hit-and-run combat  
- High bleed stacking  
- Safe mid-range pressure  
- DoT-based boss damage  
- Stylish wuxia fan animations  
- High agility & evasion  

The Panacea Fan has low base damage per hit but excels at applying rapid status effects.

---


A top-performing **where winds meet panacea fan build** follows three principles:

### 1. **Bleed Application**
Multiple quick fan hits stack bleed rapidly.

### 2. **Mobility**
You’re always moving — weaving light attacks between dodge windows.

### 3. **Support Utility**
Debuffs and poison interactions enhance overall pressure.

### ★ Thousand-Bloom Medicine Fan (Legendary)
- Exceptional bleed scaling

Any of these can anchor your **where winds meet panacea fan build**.

---


These skills perfectly complement bleed stacking and agile combat.

### **Agile Step (Core)**
- Reduces dodge stamina usage  
- Enables continuous movement  

### **Wind Dance**
- Enhances dash speed  
- Helps maintain bleed uptime  

### **Venom Qi Art**
- Adds poison buildup to fan strikes  
- Synergizes extremely well with bleed builds  

### **Focused Qi Slash (Optional)**
- Provides burst finishing damage  

These internal skills define the modern **where winds meet panacea fan build** meta.

---


Focus on agility and internal strength.

### Recommended Distribution:
- **Agility (Primary)** — faster attacks + higher crit chance  
- **Internal Strength (Secondary)** — bleed/poison scaling  
- **Vitality (Optional)** — comfort HP for boss fights  

Avoid heavy vitality stacking — mobility is your real defense.

---


The Panacea Fan Bleed Build revolves around hit-and-run patterns.

### Basic Rotation
1. Dash-in  
2. Light Fan Combo × 3 (stack bleed)  
3. Dodge-cancel to side  
4. Apply Venom Qi Art  
5. Reapply fan hits  
6. Retreat & let bleed tick  

### Boss Burst Rotation
- Perfect Dodge → Agile Step reset → Rapid Fan Barrage → Qi Slash  

The bleed stacks do most of the damage over time.

### Crowd Control Rotation
- Fan Throw → Sweep Arc → Side-dash → Bleed Spread  

A refined **where winds meet panacea fan build** deals damage while staying nearly untouchable.

---


### ★ Poison Infusion  
- Best synergy for bleed builds  
- Double DoT pressure  

### ★ Wind Infusion  
- Enhances speed & combo flow  

### ★ Ice Infusion  
- Helps control fast bosses  

Avoid Earth infusion — it doesn’t benefit light-hitting weapons.

- Never stand still — movement is your main defense.  
- Bleed doesn’t require constant attacking; stack and reposition.  
- Poison + bleed multiplies pressure on bosses.  
- Use diagonal dodges instead of direct backsteps.  
- Disable motion blur for clearer enemy telegraphs.  

---


### Strong Against:
- Humanoid bosses  
- Fast enemies with low armor  
- Multi-target encounters  

### Weak Against:
- High-armor brutes  
- Enemies immune to status effects  

Still, mobility gives the build strong adaptability.
