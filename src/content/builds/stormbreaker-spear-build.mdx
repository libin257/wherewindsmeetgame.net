The Stormbreaker Spear is designed for players who want:

- Long-range safety  
- Lightning-infused burst potential  
- Reliable control over fast-moving enemies  
- Strong single-target DPS  
- Mid-range mobility  

A properly optimized **where winds meet stormbreaker spear build** excels at maintaining distance while delivering powerful elemental attacks that interrupt, shock, and overwhelm enemies.

# Combat Strategy & Rotation

This build is all about timing thrusts and managing distance.

### Basic Rotation
1. Maintain mid-range  
2. Poke with light thrusts  
3. Dash sideways to avoid retaliation  
4. Lightning Qi Burst → heavy thrust  
5. Reset spacing  

### Burst Rotation (Boss Damage)
- Lightning Qi Burst → Spear Charge → Stormstrike Combo → Heavy Follow-Up  

This yields high shock buildup and burst severity.

### Crowd-Control Rotation
- Sweeping Halberd Arc → Lightning Pulse → Dash Reset  

The **where winds meet stormbreaker spear build** provides excellent area suppression.

# Elemental Infusions

For this spear, lightning is king.

### ★ Lightning Infusion (Best)
- Highest burst potential  
- Boosts shock damage  
- Perfect for this spear’s identity  

### Wind Infusion
- Helps movement speed  
- Good secondary option  

### Ice Infusion
- Adds slow and control  
- Useful for boss fights  

Avoid Earth infusion — spear doesn’t benefit from slow, heavy swings.

# Advanced Tips

- Keep enemies at the tip of your spear — avoid getting too close.  
- Lightning windows reward patience; don’t burnout stamina.  
- Disable motion blur for clearer visual telegraphs.  
- Dash diagonally instead of straight backward for better spacing.  
- Charge attacks only when enemies enter predictable animations.
