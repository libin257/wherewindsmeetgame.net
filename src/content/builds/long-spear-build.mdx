## Introduction

The Long Spear excels in controlling space, interrupting enemy momentum, and minimizing risk for players who prefer a measured and tactical approach. Its long reach allows you to keep enemies at bay, while its defensive moveset enables tank-focused strategies that absorb damage and punish openings.

This **where winds meet long spear build** emphasizes:

- High survivability  
- Reliable mid-range pokes  
- Stagger/interrupt potential  
- Defensive internal skill synergy  
- Simple but effective gameplay flow  

If you prefer safe, structured combat rather than fast, risky DPS styles, this build is perfect for you.

---


A Long Spear tank setup is ideal for players who enjoy:

- Staying alive in long boss fights  
- Keeping enemies at a distance  
- Controlling battle tempo  
- Acting as a frontline shield in group content  
- Counterattacking safely  

Compared to light melee weapons, the Long Spear provides:

- Superior range  
- High interrupt potential  
- Safe damage windows  
- Easy spacing control  
- High posture damage  

This gives the **where winds meet long spear build** one of the safest playstyles in the game.

---


A tank spear build relies on durability and internal strength.

### Recommended Distribution:
- **Vitality (Primary)** — max HP + survivability  
- **Internal Strength (Secondary)** — improves spear scaling  
- **Agility (Optional)** — minor mobility boosts  

You do not need high agility; your tank identity is about surviving, not speed.

---


The Long Spear Tank build plays slowly and methodically.

### Basic Rotation
1. Maintain mid-range  
2. Poke with light thrusts  
3. Block or backstep heavy attacks  
4. Counter-thrust after enemy recovery  
5. Repeat calmly  

### Heavy Counter Rotation
- Block an attack → Guarded Qi Flow activates → Heavy Thrust → Stability Pulse  

This sequence deals great stagger damage.

### Group Play Rotation
- Stay in front  
- Hold aggro  
- Use long sweeps to interrupt adds  
- Allow allies to unload DPS safely  

The **where winds meet long spear build** truly shines in team content.

---


The Spear pairs best with elements that enhance control and survivability.

### Recommended Elements:
- **Ice** — slows enemies, perfect for tanking  
- **Earth** — improves defensive scaling  
- **Wind** — increases spacing and mid-range poke speed  

Avoid Lightning infusion — it’s high burst with low tank synergy.

- You don’t need to attack often — win through patience.  
- Keep enemies at spear-tip distance to reduce danger.  
- Learn enemy attack rhythms — block the heavy hits, poke the weak ones.  
- Disable motion blur to read telegraphs more clearly.  
- Never spam dodges — your strength is stability, not mobility.  

---


### Strong Against:
- Large beasts  
- Slow humanoid bosses  
- Enemies weak to stagger  
- Multi-add encounters  

### Weaker Against:
- Extremely fast duelists  
- Bosses with long-range magical AoEs  

Still, with patience, the Long Spear can survive nearly anything.
