This **where winds meet meteor hammer build** guide breaks down one of the most devastating heavy weapons in *Where Winds Meet*. The Meteor Hammer delivers massive impact damage, long reach, crowd control, and explosive finishing moves. It’s a weapon for players who enjoy crushing blows, powerful momentum swings, and controlling entire groups of enemies with wide-area attacks.

## Introduction

The Meteor Hammer is a heavy, chain-linked weapon that excels at:

- High-impact AoE  
- Extreme stagger potential  
- Huge damage per hit  
- Disrupting enemy formations  
- Long-range swinging attacks  

This weapon type rewards patience, timing, and positioning rather than constant aggression. When wielded correctly, a **where winds meet meteor hammer build** can dominate both bosses and crowds with overwhelming force.

## Why Choose a Meteor Hammer Build?

Meteor Hammer users tend to enjoy playstyles based on:

- **Big, satisfying hits**  
- **Breaking boss posture quickly**  
- **Controlling wide areas of the battlefield**  
- **High burst damage windows**  
- **Crowd control and enemy manipulation**  

This build is perfect for players who don’t mind slower animations in exchange for enormous payoff.

## Core Build Philosophy

A successful **where winds meet meteor hammer build** rests on three pillars:

### 1. Impact  
Your goal is to make every hit count — this weapon thrives on high-damage, high-stagger swings.

### 2. Control  
Meteor Hammer has some of the best AoE and crowd control tools in the game.

### 3. Momentum  
Build momentum through charged attacks and chain swings that force enemies into defensive patterns.

Meteor Hammer pairs well with internal skills that boost survivability and burst.

### **Earth Pulse (Core)**
- Boosts heavy attack damage  
- Enhances stagger potential  

### **Iron Body Qi**
- Prevents interruptions during charge attacks  
- Essential for tankier heavy weapon players  

### **Focused Breath**
- Restores stamina rapidly  
- Useful due to high stamina requirements  

### **Wind Dance (Optional)**
- Helps compensate for slow movement  
- Strong mobility upgrade  

These skills form the backbone of the **where winds meet meteor hammer build** meta.

---


Meteor Hammer builds rely on strength and durability.

### Recommended Attributes:
- **Internal Strength (Primary)** — maximizes impact scaling  
- **Vitality (Secondary)** — boosts survivability during long wind-ups  
- **Agility (Low Investment)** — only if you want slightly faster dodges  

The weapon itself handles damage — you focus on living and landing the hits.

---


This is a deliberate, heavy weapon that rewards patience.

### Basic Rotation
1. Start at mid-range  
2. Swing to force enemy movement  
3. Follow with a heavy charged attack  
4. Step back to reset spacing  
5. Re-engage when enemy telegraphs  

### Boss Burst Rotation
- Earth Pulse → Charge Swing → Meteor Slam → Heavy Chain Sweep  

This sequence deals colossal posture damage.

### Crowd Control Rotation
- Wide Arc Swing → Step-and-Pull Chain → Spinning Slam  

The **where winds meet meteor hammer build** is particularly effective in multi-enemy encounters.

---


Meteor Hammer benefits strongly from earth and lightning.

### Recommended Infusions:
- **Earth** — boosts stagger and heavy impact  
- **Lightning** — increases burst damage  
- **Ice** — enhances crowd control and boss slowdown  

Avoid Wind infusion — it doesn’t complement the heavy swing animations.

- Anticipate enemy movement — don’t swing blindly.  
- Charge attacks only when an enemy is committed to an animation.  
- Disable motion blur to read telegraphs clearly.  
- Don’t spam dodges; use controlled steps to reposition.  
- Meteor Hammer excels at punishing after big enemy attacks — be patient.  

---


### Strong Against:
- Slow, large bosses  
- Multi-add encounters  
- Enemies weak to posture damage  

### Weak Against:
- Fast duelists  
- Teleporting enemies  
- Ranged spellcasters  

Still, with practice, Meteor Hammer players can overcome almost any encounter.
