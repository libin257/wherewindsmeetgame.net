## Introduction

Dual Blades specialize in quick multi-hit attacks, excellent dodge-cancel options, and burst damage windows that melt bosses when executed well. Compared to single Blades, Dual Blades have:

- Faster attack strings  
- Higher combo complexity  
- Stronger mobility synergy  
- Lower individual hit damage  
- Higher crowd control potential  

This **where winds meet dual blades build** is all about momentum, positioning, and reading enemy animations so you can weave in long combos without taking unnecessary hits.

## Why Choose Dual Blades?

A Dual Blades setup is ideal for players who enjoy:

- Extreme agility  
- High crit rate potential  
- Repositioning mid-combo  
- Fluid movement-based combat  
- Precise punish windows  

The main weaknesses are low base damage per hit and vulnerability to big AoE attacks, but with the proper build, Dual Blades become one of the strongest PvE and PvP weapons.

## Core Build Philosophy

An optimal **where winds meet dual blades build** revolves around three concepts:

### 1. Speed Over Power  
Every attack should flow into the next — no wasted frames.

### 2. Repositioning  
Dual Blades let you attack while moving, which keeps you safe during boss patterns.

### 3. Critical Pressure  
Multi-hit attacks generate massive crit opportunities.

## Recommended Internal Skills

Dual Blades rely heavily on mobility and stamina efficiency. These internal skills synergize extremely well:

### Agile Step (Core)
- Reduces dodge stamina cost  
- Allows extended combo uptime  

### Wind Dance (S-Tier)
- Boosts dash speed  
- Makes weaving attacks smoother  

### Phantom Blink
- Short-range teleport  
- Fantastic for re-engaging or dodging linear attacks  

### Focused Qi Slash  
- Adds strong burst damage  
- Best for exploiting boss recovery windows  

These skills define the upper-tier **where winds meet dual blades build** meta.

## Attribute Allocation

Dual Blades scale with agility and crit efficiency.

### Recommended Distribution:
- **Agility (Primary)** — improves crit rate and dodge speed  
- **Internal Strength (Secondary)** — better overall weapon scaling  
- **Vitality (Optional)** — for players who need extra survivability  

High stamina regeneration is crucial for extended fights.

## Combat Strategy & Rotation

A strong **where winds meet dual blades build** emphasizes flowing rotations.

### Basic Combo Flow
1. Dash-in  
2. Triple-Light Combo  
3. Dodge-cancel through the enemy  
4. Heavy Combo from behind  
5. Retreat  
6. Re-engage on enemy recovery  

### Burst Combo (Boss Execution Window)
- Parry → Phantom Blink Behind → Dual Rush → Qi Slash → Backstep  

Dual Blades excel at exploiting boss openings more than brute force.

## Elemental Infusions

Dual Blades benefit greatly from elements that enhance crit or slow enemies.

### Recommended Infusions:
- **Wind** — boosts speed and crit chains  
- **Ice** — adds slow effect, perfect vs. fast bosses  
- **Lightning** — increases burst damage during multihit combos  

Avoid Fire infusion — it doesn’t pair well with fast, low-hit-damage attacks.

## Advanced Tips

- Dual Blades reward players who stay aggressive but precise.  
- Learn enemy attack rhythms — don’t random-dodge.  
- Disable motion blur for clearer enemy movement.  
- Use diagonal dodges to avoid linear attacks.  
- Stay behind humanoid bosses to avoid frontal AoE hits.  

## Boss Matchups

### Excellent Against:
- Fast duelists  
- Agile sword fighters  
- Shadow-type bosses  
- Single-target large creatures  

### More Challenging:
- Beam/AoE casters  
- Heavy-armored brute bosses  
- Multi-add battles  

Still, with proper stamina management, Dual Blades remain highly adaptable.
