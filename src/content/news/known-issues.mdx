The game *Where Winds Meet* has garnered significant attention, and with that comes a wave of player feedback. Based on community reports, several bugs and issues have emerged that need attention. Here are some of the most commonly encountered problems:

- **Frame Rate Drops**: A significant issue for players with lower-end systems, this can occur during large-scale battles or in densely populated areas.
- **NPC Behavior Bugs**: Some players have noted that NPCs do not behave as expected, with certain quests failing to trigger or NPCs getting stuck in animations.
